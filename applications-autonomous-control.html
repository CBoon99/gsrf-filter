Why overshoot matters

In safety-critical control, crossing a bound even briefly can cause irreversible damage (mechanical limits, thermal excursions, unsafe trajectories). The goal is often bounded response rather than best average performance.

Where recursive amplification appears

  • Adaptive controllers updating gains online
  • State estimation feeding into action selection (tight coupling)
  • Optimization-in-the-loop systems with receding horizons
