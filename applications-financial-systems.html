Where recursion appears

  • Strategy parameters updated based on recent performance (self-referential)
  • Risk models feeding allocation decisions, which change the data distribution
  • Automated decision pipelines that optimize toward extrema under constraints

Why bounded updates help

Bounding update magnitude can reduce sudden divergence and can make worst-case behavior more auditable under defined assumptions.
