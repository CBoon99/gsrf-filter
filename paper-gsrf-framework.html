Abstract (plain language)

This paper frames GSRF as a structurally bounded safety layer for recursive systems. The goal is to prevent runaway amplification and overshoot by constraining updates and enforcing stability checks prior to state changes.

  • Defines the failure mode: recursive amplification under feedback.
  • Proposes bounded-by-construction constraints as prevention.
  • Discusses how stability criteria can be enforced before execution.
